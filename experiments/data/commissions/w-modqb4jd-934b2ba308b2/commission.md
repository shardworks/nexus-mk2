After this commission, the writ-type registry no longer recognizes `piece` (only `step`). Any persisted writ with `type: 'piece'` in production guild databases will fail downstream tooling that validates against registered types. The brief intentionally treats this as out-of-scope (the alias entry is documentation-only per D15), but a real guild may need a one-time migration: either rename `type` on stored writs from `piece` to `step`, or accept that such writs are now unregistered.

Follow-up: assess whether any active guild has writs of type `piece` in its `clerk/writs` book and, if so, prepare a migration commission. The Stacks `book.find` API plus a per-writ `patch` would be sufficient. Migration tooling would also be a place to actually consume `vocabulary-aliases.yaml` if obs-3's design lands.
