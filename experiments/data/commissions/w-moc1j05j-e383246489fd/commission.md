Per D23, Reckoner emits only on root-writ transitions. When a deep child fails and cascades to root, the patron's pulse will say 'root writ failed' without naming the leaf cause inline. The child's resolution text becomes the parent's `'Child "<childId>" failed: <childResolution>'` via Clerk's upward cascade, so the root's `resolution` does preserve the leaf id and reason — but the summary template is unstructured. The Reckoner should expand that string in `pulse.summary` (and include structured leaf info in `pulse.context`) to keep the patron informed. Not a new decision, just a spec-writer reminder that the summary assembly deserves attention.
