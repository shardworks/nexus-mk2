The supersede sugar produces a standard `click_links` record. To answer the operator question 'has this click been superseded?' a caller currently has to call `links(clickId)` and filter `inbound` by `linkType === 'supersedes'`. This works but the pattern is verbose enough that a small helper — `RatchetApi.supersededBy(clickId): Promise<ClickDoc[]>` returning the source clicks of inbound supersedes links — would meaningfully simplify the call sites that need it (especially the c-mobzwczn tree/extract surfacing work).

Not required for the sugar to ship, but worth flagging: the moment `c-mobzwczn` (or any tree/extract surfacing work) starts, this helper will likely be the first thing it adds. Pre-emptively adding it during this commission could shrink the next commission — but is a clear scope-creep risk and is therefore left as an observation rather than folded in.
