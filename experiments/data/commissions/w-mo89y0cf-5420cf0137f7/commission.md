Format drift between spider and animator dashboards
animator/src/static/animator.js renders session costs with 4-decimal precision (Number(costUsd).toFixed(4)), while this commission will move spider to 2 decimals per the brief. Once shipped, the two dashboards will disagree on cost precision for the same underlying SessionDoc values. Out of scope for this brief, but a future commission should decide whether animator converges to 2 decimals (likely the right call now that spider has moved) or whether this divergence is intentional. A guild-wide currency-formatting convention in a shared place would eliminate drift permanently.
