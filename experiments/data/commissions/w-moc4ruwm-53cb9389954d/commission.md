`ChangeEvent` in `packages/plugins/stacks/src/types.ts:117–136` carries `entry` and `prev` but no sequence number, CDC-event id, or dispatch timestamp. Observers that want to dedupe replays must infer identity from document fields (this commission uses `entry.updatedAt`). If Stacks ever gains at-least-once Phase 2 delivery, an explicit event-id in ChangeEvent would remove the per-observer dedupe dance. Not in scope for this hardening commission, but worth recording as a Stacks-side concern.
