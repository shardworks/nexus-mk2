On the main writs page:

- Toggle to hide/show non-root writs (show by default)
- Roots should be displayed beneath their parents, with an appropriate indentation level, in the writs table
- Writ table sorting affects the root writs, since children are always displayed under their parent

On a writ's detail page:

- If the writ has a parent, it should be shown and linked to
- If the writ has children, they should be displayed in a table on a writs page. The writ rows should be clickable, to navigate to the details
