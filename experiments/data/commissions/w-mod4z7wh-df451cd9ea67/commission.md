Today's `tryCollect` rate-limit path clears `engine.sessionId` when transitioning the engine to blocked (spider.ts L1533). Under the new model, a retryable failure leaves the engine as pending + holdUntil, and the old sessionId is now irrelevant to the in-flight slot (but is recorded in attempts[]). Implementation should explicitly drop the top-level `sessionId` when scheduling a retry and confirm no read-site relies on stale sessionIds being present on pending engines.
