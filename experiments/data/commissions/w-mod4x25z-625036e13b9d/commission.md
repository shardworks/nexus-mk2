`packages/plugins/astrolabe/pages/astrolabe/astrolabe.js` L381 fetches `/api/rig/list?limit=100` to find the rig matching a planId. If the plan's rig sits outside the latest 100 (older plan with no recent activity), `rigs.find(r => r.writId === planId)` will miss and the cost summary will silently render `Cost data not available`. The dashboard then has no way to show cost for older plans. Consider switching this lookup to `/api/rig/for-writ?writId=<planId>` (the existing `rigForWritTool`) which is direct and limit-free.
