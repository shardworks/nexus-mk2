`SpiderStuckCause = 'failed-blocker' | 'cycle'` in `spider/src/types.ts`. Reckoner's `emitStuck` writes `stuckCause` as `string` to the pulse context (typed loosely); lattice-discord renders `ctx.stuckCause` as `string` after a `typeof === 'string'` guard.
