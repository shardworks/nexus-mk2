`reckonings-book.md` declares `tickEventId?: string` on the record schema and §'Tick identity' explains that the Reckoner has 'two trigger paths' — scheduled tick (Clockworks `schedule:` standing order) and CDC-driven. v0 of the CDC handler ships only the CDC-driven path; no scheduled tick is wired. Records produced by this commission will always have `tickEventId === undefined`. The schema field is forward-compatible — future Reckoner-core scheduling work will populate it — but the absence of the scheduling path means deferred writs have no wake-up mechanism in v0 (the `deferUntil` / `deferSignal` fields would also go unused). With the always-approve stub (D4), this is a non-issue; with any other stub it would surface as a stuck-defer-forever pathology. Worth a comment in the apparatus docstring when this commission lands.
