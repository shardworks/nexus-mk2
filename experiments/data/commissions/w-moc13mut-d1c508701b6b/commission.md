`packages/plugins/astrolabe/src/engines/observation-lift.ts` is not idempotent today — the loop posts one writ per observation and retains no correlation between plandoc observation ids and created writ ids. If the engine fails partway through (either on `clerk.post` or, after this commission, on `clerk.link`) and is ever re-run, it will post duplicate draft writs for observations whose posts already succeeded. This pre-dates the dependency-link change and widens slightly with it (link failures become a second fail-point per observation). A follow-up could correlate `observation.id` with created writ metadata (e.g. an `observationWritIds: Record<string, string>` slot on `PlanDoc`, or outbound links labelled by observation id on the brief) so a re-run can skip observations with a known child writ. Out of scope for this brief; noted here in case retry clockwork is later extended to replay this engine.
