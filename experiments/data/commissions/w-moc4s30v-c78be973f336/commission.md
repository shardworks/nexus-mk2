Three of five existing copies of the helper use the bare `id.split('-').slice(0, 2).join('-')`; two (Ratchet's `click-tree.ts` line 18, Clerk's `writ-tree.ts` line 25) use a guarded `parts.length >= 2 ? slice : id` form. The two forms produce identical output for every input the codebase generates (and for empty strings, single tokens, and the `'-'` edge case). The guard suggests an edge that does not exist. The current commission resolves this by collapsing both into a single bare implementation in `@shardworks/nexus-core`; observation captures the past pattern in case a future reader wonders why the guarded form ever existed.
