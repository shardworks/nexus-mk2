This commission opts to enrich the existing `reckoner.writ-failed` pulse rather than introduce a dedicated `reckoner.engine-exhausted` trigger (per D1). The dedicated-trigger path was the design click `c-mod06b5m`'s original framing and has merits not chosen here: distinct color in Discord, distinct subscribe/filter for ops integrations, channel-level routing rules, and clearer semantics for consumers that want to count engine-driven failures specifically.

Follow-up: revisit when a second use-case for trigger-level discrimination appears (e.g. dashboards filtering only engine-driven failures, or per-trigger rate-limit policies on the Lattice). Cost of moving from enriched-context to dedicated-trigger later is small — the engine-context fields can stay on writ-failed during a deprecation window.
