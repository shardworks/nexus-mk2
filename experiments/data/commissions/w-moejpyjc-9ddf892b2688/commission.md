`packages/plugins/astrolabe/src/astrolabe.ts:400-425` registers `step` and `observation-set` configs with no `childrenBehavior` block at all. Astrolabe's `step` writs can have child steps (the dynamic-graft path in spider does post step-children of step-parents); when a parent step is cancelled today, child steps are silently abandoned. Once the `parentTerminal` schema field exists, astrolabe could opt step into the cascade with `parentTerminal: { transition: 'cancelled', resolution: 'Parent step cancelled' }`. Out of scope for this commission (the brief is mandate-focused), but worth a follow-up commission once the schema lands. observation-set similarly could opt in (and probably should — cancelling an obs-set should cancel its draft mandates).
