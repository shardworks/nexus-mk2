`packages/plugins/clerk/src/tools/writ-show.ts` (and the underlying ClerkApi) returns `children.summary` (counts per phase) over direct children only. With a hierarchy apparatus coming online, animas and patrons inspecting a parent writ may expect a subtree-wide rollup ("this parent has 42 descendants across 3 open, 35 completed, 4 stuck"). Today the subtree size can only be computed by walking `writ-list?parentId=` manually. A future commission could add a `summary.deep` counter or expose a `writ-tree` mode that returns just counts.
