The brief deliberately keeps tree/extract rendering changes out of scope, but it's worth naming what that means in practice: `renderMarkdown` at `packages/plugins/ratchet/src/ratchet.ts:151-177` will continue to render only the current goal line for each click. Extract's markdown output (produced by `clicks-extract`) therefore presents an amended click's goal as if it were the original, with no breadcrumb that the question has evolved. A future commission could add a `Prior goals: N` line in `renderMarkdown` when `goalHistory?.length > 0`, and a `--include-history` flag on `click-extract` that expands the prior-goal entries inline.
