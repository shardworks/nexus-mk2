Spider's `autoUnstick()` only releases writs stuck via `failed-blocker` and `cycle` causes (spider.ts L2257). Writs stuck via the dormant `engine-failure` cause before this commission lands won't auto-release under the new model — clockworks-retry's trigger is required to move them back to `open`, and that trigger stops firing after cap-reached. Operators may end up with pre-commission stuck writs they can't escape without manual phase transitions. A `nsg writ-rescue-stuck` tool (or a one-shot migration during deploy) should list and requeue them.
