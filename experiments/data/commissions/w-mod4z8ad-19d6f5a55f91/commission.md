Reckoner's `isTerminalStuck` predicate (predicates.ts) is built around writs in `phase='stuck'` with retry metadata. Under the new model the writ goes straight to `phase='failed'` on engine exhaustion, bypassing the predicate entirely. Without a new pulse trigger, patrons lose the Discord notification on exhausted rigs. This is the concrete follow-up that obs-2 alludes to — the Lattice work needs a `reckoner.writ-failed` or `spider.engine-retries-exhausted` trigger.
