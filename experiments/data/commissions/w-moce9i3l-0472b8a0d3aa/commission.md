`resolveLinkMetaInDom` in `packages/plugins/ratchet/pages/clicks/index.html` (around line 745) walks every outbound and inbound link target and calls `fetchLinkMeta(id)`, which in turn calls `/api/click/show` or `/api/writ/show` per target. On a click with many cross-substrate links this fans out to N sequential-ish round trips. Cached in `linkMetaCache` after first fetch, but a freshly-opened detail pane for a linky click pays the full cost every refresh. A future commission could add a batch `resolve-refs` endpoint or opportunistically pre-populate the cache from the forest / children.list responses. Not part of the amend UI, but surfaced while tracing the render pipeline.
