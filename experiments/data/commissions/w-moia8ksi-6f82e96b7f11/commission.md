Per the contract doc §6, the v0 Reckoner has only a binary `disabledSources` skip list. There is no rate-limit, no per-source quota, and no time-windowed disabling. If a petitioner accidentally emits a flood of held writs, the operator's only lever is to add the source to `disabledSources` (which leaves all current and future writs stuck in `new` indefinitely until the source is re-enabled). Future enhancement explicitly named in the contract doc (§6 'Future config surface') and brief ('Out of scope: Operational throttling beyond the simple `disabledSources` skip list'). Not landing in this commission, but worth tracking once production traffic surfaces a real flood case.
