`packages/plugins/astrolabe/src/types.ts:15` declares `[key: string]: unknown;` on the `PlanDoc` interface. This absorbs unknown keys on stored documents without type error, which is load-bearing for tolerating historic `generatedWritId` after removal — but it also silently accepts typos and accidental writes to non-declared keys across the whole PlanDoc surface. After this commission lands, the only declared load-bearing reason for the signature goes away (no more legacy field), and there may be value in tightening the signature or replacing it with an explicit `legacy?: Record<string, unknown>` sidecar that isolates drift.
