`create()` at `packages/plugins/ratchet/src/ratchet.ts:193-216` accepts any string for `goal`, including `''` and whitespace-only. Meanwhile `conclude` (line 285) and `drop` (line 305) reject empty/whitespace conclusions; this commission's `amend` also rejects them (decision D8). That leaves `create` as the sole outlier among mutating writes to user-facing string fields. A click created with empty goal will render as a blank row throughout the Oculus page, in `renderMarkdown`, and in tree output. Add `if (!params.goal || params.goal.trim() === '') throw new Error('Goal must be a non-empty string.')` to `create`, plus a test under the `describe('create', ...)` block matching the existing conclude/drop empty-string tests at `ratchet.test.ts:366-388`.
