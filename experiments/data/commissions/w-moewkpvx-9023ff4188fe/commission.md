Four append-only event-log books now exist or are being designed in the guild: clockworks `events`, clockworks `event_dispatches`, lattice `pulses`, and the proposed reckoner `reckonings`. Animator's `sessions`/`transcripts` pair is a fifth, with a slight twist (sessions has terminal-state mutation). All four pure-event books share the same convergent shape: prefixed `<letter>-<base36_ts>-<hex>` ids via `generateId(...)`, filter-shape-driven index sets, immutable-or-near-immutable rows, Phase 2 CDC attachment, and append-only-forever default retention. The pattern is convergent enough that a shared abstraction or kit-helper could be earned — e.g., `eventLogBook({ owner, name, idPrefix, indexes })` that produces the schema declaration plus a typed `append()` helper, or a documented 'Event Log Book Pattern' in `docs/architecture/`.

Out of scope for this commission (decision-supporting design doc only).
