`docs/architecture/petitioner-registration.md` §8 declares the Reckonings outcome enum as `'approve' | 'defer' | 'decline' | 'unchanged' | 'withdrawn'`. The newer and more thorough `docs/architecture/reckonings-book.md` settles on `'accepted' | 'deferred' | 'declined' | 'no-op'` and explicitly excludes `withdrawn` (petitioner-initiated withdrawals produce no row). The two docs need to be reconciled — the contract doc §8 should be updated to point to `reckonings-book.md` for the authoritative shape, or the prose should be rewritten to match. Out of scope for this commission's behavior implementation, but the next person reading the contract doc will hit the same discrepancy and have to dig the same depth this primer did.
