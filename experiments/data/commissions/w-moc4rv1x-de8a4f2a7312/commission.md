`packages/plugins/lattice-discord` currently POSTs once per dispatched pulse and has no channel-side idempotency. If the Reckoner's emit-side dedupe ever regresses, Discord will faithfully double-notify. Consider adding a second line of defence at the channel layer (e.g. a bloom filter on recent pulse ids) as a separate hardening commission — but only after the primary emit-side fix lands, to avoid hiding bugs.
