The drain check reads `writs` (counting open) and `rigs` (counting running/blocked) after a terminal transition. During rapid bursts of completions, a second completion could land before the first's drain check finishes — producing two drain pulses when one transition was the true draining moment. Stacks Phase 2 handlers run post-commit and are serialized per-book, but the drain check spans two books. The implementer should verify empirically whether this matters and, if so, guard with a lightweight dedupe (e.g. suppress drain pulses within N milliseconds of a prior drain pulse).
