`packages/framework/cli/src/program.ts` `detectPositionalId` (line 62) only promotes a Zod schema's `id` / `*Id` keys to a Commander positional when the field is **required**. The `nsg clock tick [id]` shape (optional positional) is one of the reasons this commission goes hand-written rather than auto-built. Other future tools that want `[id]` (e.g. a hypothetical `writ-show [id]` that defaults to most recent) will hit the same wall. Worth a follow-up that lifts `detectPositionalId` to also recognize an optional id field, registered as `cmd.argument('[id]', ...)` rather than `cmd.argument('<id>', ...)`. Low-risk change — the merge logic in the action handler (program.ts line 130) already gracefully handles `positionalValue === undefined`.
