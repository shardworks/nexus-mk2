Today the Reckoner exposes inspection helpers for petitioners (`isSourceRegistered`, `isSourceDisabled`, `listPetitioners`) but no analogous surface for schedulers. With the scheduler registry landing, operators have no way to ask 'which schedulers are registered?', 'which is active?', 'what is the current schedulerConfig?' without reading guild.json by hand.

A future commission could add `listSchedulers(): SchedulerDescriptor[]` and `getActiveScheduler(): SchedulerDescriptor` to ReckonerApi, plus a `nsg reckoner schedulers` CLI tool (parallel to the apparatus-doc-suggested `nsg reckoner list-petitioners` already tracked as obs-8 in `docs/architecture/apparatus/reckoner.md:341-342`). The substrate commission deliberately ships only the contract surface; the operator tooling stays separate so it can serve both petitioners and schedulers in one pass when the need arises.
