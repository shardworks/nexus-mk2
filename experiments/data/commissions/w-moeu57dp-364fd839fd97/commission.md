Engines can dynamically extend their rig at completion via the `graft` and `graftTail` fields on `SpiderEngineRunResult` (`types.ts` lines 827–830, also surfaceable from a `collect()` return via `SpiderCollectResult`). The Spider's `tryProcessGrafts` phase validates the graft (max-engines limit, duplicate ids, upstream references, cycle detection, when/yields validation) and on success appends the engines and patches downstream engines’ upstream sets to wait on `graftTail`.

This is the mechanism behind the seal engine’s recovery tail (already documented at lines 625–632) but the doc never explicitly describes the graft API surface or the `engine-grafted` CrawlResult variant.
