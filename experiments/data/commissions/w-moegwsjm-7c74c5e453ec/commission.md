`SessionResult` (types.ts:~470) and `SessionDoc` (types.ts:509) carry largely overlapping fields (status, startedAt, endedAt, durationMs, provider, exitCode, error, conversationId, providerSessionId, tokenUsage, costUsd, metadata, output, terminationTag). `toSessionDoc(result)` at animator.ts:284 copies them one-by-one. SessionResult is the in-process attached return shape; SessionDoc is the persisted-row shape — the duplication is partly intentional (the doc has extra fields like cancelHandle, lastActivityAt, authorizedTools, terminationDiagnostic that don't go on the result). Worth examining whether the 13-field overlap can be expressed as a shared interface plus per-shape extensions. Out of scope for the reducer commission; raises a separate type-cleanup concern.
