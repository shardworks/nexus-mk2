Once the cascade engine writes a structured leaf-failure record (D1=engine-publishes-record), the Reckoner becomes one of N consumers of that signal. Other future emitters (e.g. an Animator surface that surfaces 'why did this commission fail' to the patron, or a future `reckoner.writ-cancelled` trigger) would benefit from reading the same record rather than re-deriving it. Worth lifting into a shared read-helper (e.g. `clerk.getCascadeChain(writId): string[]`) so the chase-chain logic in D4 lives once.
