The new bridge plugin sits in a triangle with Stacks and Clockworks. The framework's startup warning paths (unconsumed-kit warning, missing-recommends warning, etc.) are sensitive to `consumes:` declarations and `recommends:` declarations. A small integration test — install all three plugins together, assert `guild.startupWarnings()` is empty — would catch the kind of regression where a future change drops a `consumes: ['events']` from the bridge or stops Clockworks from declaring its own `events` kit. Cheap insurance; falls under "developer-facing test that proves intent."
