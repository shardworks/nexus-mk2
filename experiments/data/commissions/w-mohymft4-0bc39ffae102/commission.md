Animator's six emit sites currently run as a fanout pattern: every terminal-write site (in-process attached, cancel path, detached `handleSessionRecord`, orphan recovery, `session-running` tool) calls the helpers at the call site, after the write succeeds, with bespoke skip-on-write-failed guards. After C4 the count drops from six emits to three (per declaration set) but the fanout shape is unchanged — 13 emission call sites become 13 call sites with shorter event-name strings.

Candidate C in `COMPLEXITY-AUDIT.md` proposes replacing the fanout with a single CDC observer on the `animator/sessions` book. With the catalog now stabilized post-C4 (three names, fixed payload shapes), the candidate becomes more attractive: the rename burden is gone, and the centralization can be done without coordinating with C1–C5 simultaneously. Confidence depends on whether Stacks CDC surfaces previous-status on the change record (a precondition the audit flags).

Files affected: `animator.ts` (recordSession, recordRunning, cancel), `session-record-handler.ts`, `startup.ts` (orphan recovery), `tools/session-running.ts`. Net delete: ~150 LOC of fanout boilerplate; net add: ~250 LOC of new CDC observer + tests.
