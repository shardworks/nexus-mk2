Decision D5 prescribes extracting source-check + priority-validate + default-fill into a shared internal helper used by both forms. Concretely: a function `validateAndFillExtRequest(extRequest: PetitionExtRequest): { source: string; priority: Priority; complexity?: ComplexityTier; payload?: unknown; labels?: Record<string, string> }` that runs (a) the registry lookup + enforceRegistration check (with the fail-loud-or-warn diagnostic at lines 776–791 of the current `petition`), (b) `validatePriorityDimensions(extRequest.priority ?? {})`, and (c) `mergePriorityWithDefaults(extRequest.priority)`. Both forms call it; the stamp-only form calls it inline before its writ-state guards (D4 input-first ordering); the create+stamp form calls it before `clerk.post()` so a malformed input never produces an orphan writ. The diagnostic shape and fail-loud paths come straight out of the existing `petition()` body; the refactor is mechanical, but landing it as a named helper rather than inline duplication is the entire point of D5.
