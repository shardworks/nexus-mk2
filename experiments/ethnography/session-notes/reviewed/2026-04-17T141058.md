## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **Name:** `spider.follows` (rejected: `precedence-gate` too long, `dependency` implies success-coupling which the meaning no longer encodes, `precedence` reads direction backwards, `sequence` reads as a list-noun not a relationship).
- **Description:** "The source writ is a precedence-successor of the target: source cannot be dispatched until the target reaches a terminal state. Consumers define their own policy for what happens on each terminal state." Pure ordering primitive; per-state policy delegated to consumer.
- **Spider's failure policy:** dependent transitions to `stuck` with stuck-reason naming the blocker. Chosen over silent-undispatchable (accretes zombie writs) and cascade-fail (destructive, worse for recovery).
- **Packaging:** meaning declaration bundled with Spider wiring in c-mo2e88aw — ~5 lines of manifest with no standalone value.
- **Retry ergonomics:** Sean strongly leans toward option 2 (consumer-side chain-walking of `retries` links during gate resolution) over option 1 (retry primitive in Clerk), because option 1 would require Clerk to know about specific meanings or transfer all links wholesale.
- **Deferred to sub-clicks:** cycle detection mechanism, re-evaluation trigger, auto-unstick policy, conjunctive-gating confirmation, stuck-reason format.
