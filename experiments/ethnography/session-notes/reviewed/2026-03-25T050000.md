## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **Clockworks = Pillar 5 (replaces "The Pulse")** — The Pulse was a placeholder; Clockworks is the named, designed thing.
- **Standing orders** as the vocabulary for event subscriptions (not "triggers" or "hooks"). Lives in `guild.json` under `clockworks`, not in engine descriptors — guild policy, not engine metadata.
- **`run` / `summon` / `brief`** as the three standing order verbs (all verbs, consistent). `run` for engine orders; `summon`/`brief` for anima orders with different framing delivered to the anima.
- **`signal` as the verb/tool name** for emitting events; "event" retained as the noun.
- **Standing orders target roles, not named animas** — durable across anima turnover.
- **Clockworks does not create tasks** — it dispatches; animas create tasks from their own judgment.
- **Phase 1: manual `nsg clock` commands** — not auto-run after every CLI invocation. Operator-driven until trusted. `list`, `tick [id]`, `run`.
- **Static engines vs. clockwork engines** — static = bespoke APIs, framework-internal; clockwork = `engine()` factory, triggerable by standing orders.
- **Deferred:** natural language trigger syntax, pre-event hooks, payload schema enforcement, Phase 2 daemon, scheduled standing orders.
