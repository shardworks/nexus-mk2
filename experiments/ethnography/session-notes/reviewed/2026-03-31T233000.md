## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **Kit is an open record** ‚Äî `type Kit = { recommends?: string[], [key: string]: unknown }`. Framework doesn't own contribution types; apparatus packages publish their own kit interfaces (`ClockworksKit`, etc.)
- **No `ExtendedKit<T>`** ‚Äî unnecessary once Kit is open; dropped entirely
- **No `GuildRegistries` unified type** ‚Äî each apparatus maintains its own registry; Arbor doesn't aggregate across apparatus semantics
- **`ctx.kits()` / `ctx.apparatuses()` / `ctx.plugins()`** ‚Äî three snapshot methods on GuildContext; "apparatuses" is the correct English plural per project policy
- **`plugin:initialized` reinstated** ‚Äî but with a specific, motivated use: reactive consumption by apparatuses scanning kit contributions. Not the general-purpose "respond to other plugins" framing it had before
- **Async handlers** ‚Äî `start`/`stop` and lifecycle event handlers are async-capable; framework awaits sequentially. Previous "synchronous, fire-and-forget" framing dropped
- **`guild.json` key stays `"plugins"`** ‚Äî internal term; package declares its own type at load time
- **Deferred:** `anima-lifecycle.md` and `engine-designs.md` content ‚Äî held as placeholders until Walker and sessions apparatus are specced
