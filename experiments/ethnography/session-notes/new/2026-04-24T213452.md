## What we did

- **Diagnosed** rig `rig-mod69jd9-3d0c607f` (writ `w-mod644tf-236f3f2ecfc4`): implement engine stuck `running` for 40+ minutes. Anima session `ses-modd2fs5-1f17cc92` had actually been reconciled as dead at 20:31:11 ("No heartbeat received for 126s") but Spider never transitioned the engine out of running.
- **Root cause identified**: every engine in the rig carried the pre-refactor scalar schema (`sessionId`, `startedAt`, `yields` on the engine; no `attempts[]`). Post-refactor readers (`latestAttempt`, `buildUpstreamMap`, `tryCollect`, `rig-view` cost aggregation) only look at `attempts[-1]` and silently return undefined for these engines. That's why the dead session was invisible and why the first reset attempt retry-thrashed on `Cannot read properties of undefined (reading 'path')` — upstream `draft.path` wasn't reachable.
- **Recovered the rig** via direct SQLite surgery on `/workspace/vibers/.nexus/nexus.db`:
  - DB backups: `nexus.db.bak-coco-implement-unstick-20260424-2107`, `nexus.db.bak-coco-schema-migrate-20260424-2115`.
  - Lifted scalar `{sessionId, startedAt, yields}` on all 9 completed engines into synthetic `attempts: [{...}]` entries.
  - Reset implement to fresh pending; reset review/revise/seal from cancelled back to pending.
  - Cleared rig-level `terminalAt`/`cancelledAt`, status → running. Writ phase failed → open, resolution cleared.
- **Rig completed end-to-end** after the migration: implement → review → revise → seal all landed successfully. The patron-paid anima work (reader-analyst, patron-anima, spec-writer, ~$4.85 + 47K output tokens) was preserved.
