## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **Keep "engine"**: modifier pair (quick/clockwork) carries the distinction; noun doesn't mislead; switching costs exceed modest gain. The vocabulary as updated is coherent without replacing the noun.
- **Relay** adopted for Clockworks handlers — always clockwork, stimulus-response, distinct character from rig engines.
- **Quick/clockwork** adopted as the modifier pair for rig engine types. "Quick" from Early Modern English ("the quick and the dead" = alive).
- **Engine moved to Work section** of guild-metaphor.md — engines are rig workhorses, not infrastructure apparatus.
- **"Chartered" removed** from guild vocabulary — conflicts with Charter as a system term.
- **tools-and-engines.md split** into kit-components.md + anima-composition.md — better separation of concerns.
- Deferred: the engine-naming-problem.md scratch doc may be archivable now that decisions are recorded; left as-is for now.
