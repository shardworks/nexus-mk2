## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **Combined deadline for `lab.xguild-rig-terminal`** instead of
  preserving the original two-phase semantics
  (`rigDiscoveryTimeoutMs` + `timeoutMs`). Stateless gate model can't
  carry per-phase timing without mutating the condition between
  checks; the combined deadline is strictly more permissive in the
  common case.
- **Engines remain fail-fast (no retry budget)**. A transient shellout
  failure on resume terminally fails the trial. Adding retry would
  risk re-posting the commission, since `priorBlock` isn't re-supplied
  after a retry. Acceptable for now; revisit if it bites.
- **Did NOT touch the in-flight X020 scenario engine** in
  `rig-mopuhati-3d6891cb`. It was running under old code; my refactor
  takes effect on new dispatches after daemon restart only.
- `pollIntervalMs` from manifests is still validated for back-compat
  but ignored; the BlockType's own poll interval (5s) governs.
