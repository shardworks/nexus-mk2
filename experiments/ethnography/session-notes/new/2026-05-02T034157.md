  handoff to fold in distiller-cost as "another option," Sean
  asked: *"why did you update a lab handoff doc instead of

  handoff document should be readable by someone who picks up
  *that one work line* — the lab handoff shouldn't carry distiller

  embed lab context that distracts from the smaller task. The

  diagnostic — I was being asked to explain my own logic rather
  than corrected with the answer; the explanation surfaced the
  reflex. Worth remembering for future handoff authoring: one
  document per work line.
