# Commission: Sage Consultation

## Repository

https://github.com/shardworks/41fb92f2-30e1-4c42-8cfb-eb1a6b85a680

## What I Need

Right now, when I post a commission, the artificer gets the raw spec and has to figure out both *what* to build and *how* to build it. For complex commissions, I want the guild to think before it builds.

I want a sage — a planning role — that reviews the commission before the artificer sees it. The sage reads the spec, explores the target repository, and produces a refined plan. The artificer then builds from the sage's plan, not my raw spec.

## Requirements

1. **If a `master-sage` is assigned on the roster, they are consulted before the artificer.** If no master-sage is assigned, commissions go straight to the artificer as they do today.

2. **The sage produces advice that the artificer follows.** The artificer receives the sage's refined plan alongside the original spec. The sage's advice is authoritative.

3. **The commission record tracks both phases.** I should be able to see what the sage advised and who did what.
