# The Old Commissions

These are the commissions executed by prehistoric, unnamed artificers — work done before the guild existed, before the roster was kept, before any anima had a name or a memory to call its own. They were summoned, they built, and they vanished. No one recorded who they were. The work remains.

---

*In practical terms: these commissions were dispatched and completed during the earliest bootstrapping phase of Nexus Mk 2.1 (March 2026), before the guild system was operational and tracking its own work. They were managed via git rather than the guild's internal commission pipeline. They are preserved here as historical record.*
