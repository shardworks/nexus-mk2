## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **Three books, not one:** Register (who), Ledger (what work), Daybook (what happened). Clockworks owns its own state (events, dispatches) — not a book.
- **guild.json stays unnamed** in the metaphor. "Charter" was considered but rejected — "the tools we have" isn't charter material. It's just config.
- **Storage stays as one SQLite file.** The split is conceptual/documentary, not physical. Cross-book FKs and transactional consistency make physical splitting costly for no current gain.
- **CLI stays flat.** No `nsg register`/`nsg ledger` subcommand grouping. Commands are verb-oriented.
- **Avoid hardcoding "three books"** — say "the guild's Books" to leave room for future additions.
- **Deferred:** `nsg status` enhancement to show book summaries from database (spec item 8 — the only functional change, could be a separate commission).
