## Decisions made and/or deferred

- **`blocked` is a new commission status** — distinct from failed/cancelled, part of the lifecycle
- **Agent exits after blocking** — no hanging sessions; posts question via CLI, then stops. Resume dispatches a fresh session with full thread context
- **Resume targets the named anima** — commission tracks which anima is working on it; that's who gets re-dispatched
- **Multi-anima hand-offs left open** — the commission flagged the sage→artificer pipeline as needing consideration but didn't prescribe a solution. Valdris punted it to `blockedInPhase` tracking without full resume logic. Fine for v1.
